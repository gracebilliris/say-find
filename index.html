About Say Find

About the Game

Say Find is a fun party game combining knowledge, observation, and competition. Teams race to find all the answers on a themed list. Harder answers are worth more points—the team with the most points wins!

How to Play

  • Divide into teams and choose a team name and color
  • Select categories for the game
  • Each team takes turns finding answers from the list
  • More difficult answers are worth more points
  • Most points after all rounds wins!
